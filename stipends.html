Scholarship

We understand that paying the tuition could be difficult for some students wishing to enrol. And we agree that the best students deserve to study without paying the full tuition. Therefore, we are preparing two types of scholarships for which students may apply:

  • Scholarship for the students with socio-economic needs,
  • Scholarship for students' excellency.

Academic year 2023/24 is going to be the first time the program Science is opened. We are actively searching for sponsors. As of Nov 2022 we already have 10 scholarships and we believe that we will be able to increase this number by the time scholarships are being awarded.

Scholarships will be provided by

  • Institute of Organic Chemistry and Biochemistry,
  • Institute of Physical Chemistry,
  • Institute of Physics.

Moreover, there are many external foundations that provide scholarships in general

  • Bakala Foundation,
  • Nabla Foundation,
  • Kelner Foundation.

Strategy

Curriculum of Science program consists of two parts:

  • Interdisciplinary semesters 1–4
    Students will get a solid background in mathematics and informatics based on total of 8 courses they will have to pass. Physics, chemistry and biology are each represented by 4 courses and one laboratory works. Soft skills will be also addressed within courses and seminars.
  • Highly specialized semesters 5–6
    Students will build their own curriculum based on the direction they will choose for their carrier in Science. They will work on the bachelor thesis under the guidance of experts from Faculty of Mathematics and Physics, Faculty of Science or in collaboration with experts from Academy of Sciences of the Czech Republic.
